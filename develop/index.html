About

In recent times, the world has seen a big number of bad news: 2020 was a tough year with its COVID-19 pandemic, lockdown, and stress. Apart from that, news articles tend to draw readers' attention to negative sides of the situation rather than positive ones (https://www.nytimes.com/2021/03/24/world/covid-coverage-by-the-us-national-media-is-an-outlier-a-study-finds.html). Goodnewsly is a tool which serves to make good news easier to find so that it does not seem like the world is only facing problems.

Purpose

Goodnewsly is a tool which helps you find the most positive stories on the topic you are interested in. A few recent studies have found that news are overwhelmingly negative, and hence, by absorbing too much information from popular news sources, Americans often damage their mental health as a long-term effect. Goodnewsly will help you find a way in a news world: choose a topic, add a category, input the key word and enjoy the most recent positive stories!
